What is SocialShade

SocialShade is open platform for questions and answer, you can ask anything here and it will be answered by random (yet appropriate) anonymus user. Your identity is secured. It works on browser login, your browser is your authentication.

To add new question:
You can add any subjective questions, go to www.socialshade.com/ask, and type your question.



You can make question objective by adding options to choose (Click "Add answer to choose"), all objective question can be answered subjectively as user will get option "I have something else to say".



Answer other's question
Go to www.socialshade.com and answer questions, you can go to next suggested questions from right side bar



More options are available from top left menu
